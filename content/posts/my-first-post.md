# Hello World!

This is my first post on my new personal website. Im making this site to share my thoughts about stuff, experiences on technology, programming, and other interests. Maybe this will also serve as a part of my _second brain_.

I, though not as brilliant as them, want to do the same, at least for myself to keep track of my learning journey and share with others if they find it useful. The contents will be written mostly in English, but I may write in Vietnamese sometimes as well.
